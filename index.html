Our Mission

At Grace Gospel Church-Webuye, our mission is to teach, train, equip and send believers to live and proclaim the gospel of Jesus Christ with power and purpose. We are committed to grounding lives in God's word, nurturing spiritual growth and raising disciples who reveal Christ in communities and beyond. Through sound teaching, practical training and spirit-led equipping, we prepare believers to impact society with godly values and love. Our calling is to send out faithful witnesses who transform lives, restore hope and advance the kingdom of God both locally and globally for His glory. (PHILEMON 1:11)

Our Vision
